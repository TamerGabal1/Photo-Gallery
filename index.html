Cultural Experience

by Philip Hadiwidjaja and Tamer Gabal

Our Mission

The main purpose of creating this website is to spread culture through pictures. Through the different albums, we attempt to flesh out the complexities and differences of lifestyle, culture, and regions, that we sometimes ignore. Living in New York City, we tend to forget the more unknown and unpopularized areas of different countries. Being Indonesian, I wanted to express the beauty that Indonesia has in its land, while also addressing the issues that disrupt its beauty.
